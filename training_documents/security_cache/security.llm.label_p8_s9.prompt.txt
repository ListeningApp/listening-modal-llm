0fpw | One of the common models of security management has been 'Threat-Based Management' which was covered in the three-stage Risk Assessment - Risk Control - Contingency Planning model introduced in Section 1.2, which is based on the idea that if you identify all possible threats, and then develop methods ('protocols') to prevent them from happening, you will then have created a safe organisation.
